About OSCR

OSCR stands for **Online Sex Crime Reporters**. We are a dedicated group focused on reporting online sexual crimes in the most responsible, safe, and effective way. By using proper channels and ensuring accurate reporting, we aim to protect vulnerable individuals and maintain the safety of the online community. Our goal is to work alongside authorities and relevant organizations to ensure swift action and justice for victims of online sex crimes. We emphasize the importance of responsible reporting, legal compliance, and the protection of personal data to ensure the safety of both victims and reporters.
